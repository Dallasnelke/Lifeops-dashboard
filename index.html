About This Project

            
LifeOps is a personal operating dashboard that connects money, nutrition, fitness, wellness, and long-term goals in one place.

            

              
What it does
Tracks daily habits, macro progress, food cost, weekly actions, wellness signals, money health, and goal progress.

              
Who it is for
People trying to build a healthier, more stable life without juggling separate apps for food, money, habits, and goals.

              
Problem it solves
Most trackers show one part of life. LifeOps shows how choices connect across budget, health, energy, and progress.

            

          


          

            
Why It Is Different

            

              
More than food tracking
It keeps food logging and macro visibility, then adds meal cost, savings pressure, wellness, actions, and goals.

              
More than budget tracking
It treats money as one life signal, not the whole picture. Food, sleep, workouts, stress, and priorities are part of the same weekly review.

              
Portfolio value
This demonstrates dashboard design, KPI scoring, user workflows, local data storage, trend tracking, and practical product thinking.

            

          


          

            
Future AI Roadmap

            

              
AI weekly coach
Summarize the biggest win, biggest risk, weakest area, and three realistic actions for next week.

              
AI pattern detection
Explain why a score changed by comparing food cost, protein, sleep, workouts, and completed actions over time.

              
AI meal and budget guidance
Suggest practical meals that support protein goals without breaking the weekly food budget.

            

          


          

            
Employer Walkthrough

            

              
Business question
What is the next best action when money, health, and goals compete for attention?

              
Dashboard answer
A single LifeOps score, clean category health cards, trend history, weekly review, and exportable report.

              
Next build phase
Turn the rule-based coach into a real AI assistant once the app has enough history to give useful context.

            

          


          

            
Case Study

            

              
Problem
People make food, money, fitness, and wellness decisions in separate tools, so tradeoffs are easy to miss.

              
Solution
LifeOps combines key daily inputs, category scores, history, reports, and action planning into one practical dashboard.

              
Features
Onboarding, macro and cost logging, money setup, wellness check-ins, trends, weekly actions, reports, and coach preview.

              
Skills demonstrated
Dashboard UX, KPI design, state management, local storage, export workflows, product positioning, and business analysis.

              
Future roadmap
Real AI coaching, better meal database tools, richer trend analysis, account integrations, and PDF-quality report exports.
